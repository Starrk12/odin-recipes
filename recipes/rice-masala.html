Rice masala

This beef pilau rice is a mouth watering rice dish cooked in a broth, seasoned with oriental spices. It's easy to make and tastes so yummy.

These recipes use fresh ingredients but you can use pilau masala powder or pilau mix instead of some of the spices if you do not have time to use fresh ingredients. These are the spices in pilau masala cloves, black peppercorn, cardamom, cinnamon and cumin.

ingridients

  • 1 cup of beef fillet cubed
  • 3 red onions, cubed
  • 1 tablespoon garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon cardamon pods

Procedure

  1. STEP 1: ADD THE ONIONS AND WHOLE SPICES INTO THE PAN AND COOK THE UNTIL ONIONS ARE TRANSLUCENT.
  2. STEP 2: ADD THE OTHER SPICES AND TOAST FOR 15 MINUTES ON LOW.
  3. STEP 3: SHORTLY AFTER, ADD THE BEEF AND COOK DOWN UNTIL BROWNED SLIGHTLY.
  4. STEP 6: ADD 5 CUPS OF BOILING WATER AND COVER THE RICE AND COOK IT UNTIL ALL WATER HAS EVAPORATED AND RICE HAS COOKED THROUGH.
